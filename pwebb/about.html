About Me

Hello! My name is Andy Ko, and I am a freshman at Duke University where I will be studying Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science. As I moved from Korea to Canada and then to the United States, I always pushed myself to learn new languages and the means to communicate with others. I've also worked as a translator for my parents and non-anglophone families throughout my journey. I took an interest in computer languages, especially after taking an online introductory course in Python. I plan to utilize my computer science knowledge to become a translator that breaks the barriers between technology and humans. Outside of my education, I also play the clarinet and tennis.
